The internet is a vast and dynamic space that connects billions of people and devices around the world. There are millions of websites that offer various kinds of information, services, entertainment, and communication. However, some websites are more popular and influential than others, attracting a large number of visitors and generating a lot of traffic. This article will show you some of the most-visited websites in the world, based on data from Semrush and Similarweb, two leading web analytics platforms.

How are the most-visited websites ranked?

There are different ways to measure and rank the popularity and traffic of websites, such as the number of unique visitors, page views, sessions, time spent, bounce rate, etc. However, one of the most common and widely used metrics is the monthly visits, which is the total number of visits to a website in a given month, regardless of how many times a visitor returns or how many pages they view. This metric reflects the overall reach and exposure of a website to the online audience.

However, measuring and comparing the monthly visits of websites is not a simple task, as different sources may use different methods and tools to collect and analyze the data. Therefore, the rankings and numbers may vary depending on the source and the time period. In this article, we will use the data from Semrush and Similarweb, two reputable and reliable web analytics platforms that offer comprehensive and up-to-date insights into the web traffic and performance of websites. Both platforms use a combination of direct measurement, panel data, web crawlers, and third-party sources to estimate the monthly visits of websites.

What are the most-visited websites in the world?

According to the data from Semrush and Similarweb, as of December 2023, the top 10 most-visited websites in the world are:

  1. Google.com: The leading and most popular search engine in the world, offering various products and services such as Gmail, Google Maps, Google Drive, Google Photos, Google Translate, Google Chrome, Google Ads, Google Analytics, Google Cloud, and more.
  2. YouTube.com: The largest and most popular video-sharing and streaming platform in the world, offering a wide range of content such as music, movies, TV shows, live events, gaming, education, news, sports, and more.
  3. Facebook.com: The largest and most popular social media network in the world, offering various features and services such as Messenger, Instagram, WhatsApp, Marketplace, Groups, Pages, Events, Watch, Stories, News Feed, and more.
  4. Instagram.com: The most popular photo and video-sharing social media platform in the world, offering various features and services such as Reels, IGTV, Stories, Live, Explore, Shop, and more.
  5. Twitter.com: The most popular microblogging and social media platform in the world, offering various features and services such as Tweets, Retweets, Likes, Replies, Direct Messages, Trends, Moments, Fleets, Spaces, and more.
  6. Baidu.com: The leading and most popular search engine in China, offering various products and services such as Baidu Maps, Baidu Cloud, Baidu Baike, Baidu Tieba, Baidu Wangpan, Baidu Translate, Baidu Ads, Baidu Analytics, Baidu AI, and more.
  7. Wikipedia.org: The largest and most popular online encyclopedia in the world, offering free and reliable information on various topics and languages, edited and maintained by volunteers and supported by donations.
  8. Yahoo.com: One of the oldest and most popular web portals and search engines in the world, offering various products and services such as Yahoo Mail, Yahoo News, Yahoo Finance, Yahoo Sports, Yahoo Entertainment, Yahoo Answers, Yahoo Shopping, and more.
  9. Yandex.ru: The leading and most popular search engine and web portal in Russia, offering various products and services such as Yandex Mail, Yandex Disk, Yandex Maps, Yandex Translate, Yandex Browser, Yandex Ads, Yandex Metrica, Yandex Cloud, Yandex AI, and more.
  10. WhatsApp.com: The most popular and widely used messaging and voice-over-IP service in the world, offering various features and services such as text, voice, and video calls, group chats, status updates, media sharing, end-to-end encryption, and more.
